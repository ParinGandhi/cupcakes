About

My name is Payal Gandhi, and baking has always been more than a passion—it has been a way to bring joy to others through thoughtful, delicious treats. As a vegetarian who does not eat eggs, I KNOW how difficult it can be to find desserts that are both satisfying and aligned with specific dietary needs. My baking journey became even more personal as I learned to create incredible desserts without eggs, while also adapting recipes to be nut-free due to my son's allergies.

As a mother of two wonderful children, I have spent years discovering creative techniques and high-quality ingredients that make it possible to bake desserts that are flavorful, inclusive, and made with care.
